Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Log in to your patient dashboard and click on "Book an Appointment" from the sidebar. Choose your preferred doctor, date, and time slot, then confirm.

How can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Go to "Appointments" in your dashboard. Select the appointment you wish to change, and you'll find options to reschedule or cancel.

Where can I view my medical reports?

Your medical reports are available under "My Records" section. You can view or download your lab results, prescriptions, and previous consultations.

How do I contact support?

Use the "Contact Us" form in the dashboard footer or email us at support@yourclinic.com. Our team is available 24x7.
