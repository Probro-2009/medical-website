Sinusitis: The Hidden Battle Behind Your Nose

Sinusitis occurs when the sinuses become inflamed due to infections, allergies, or environmental factors. This condition leads to symptoms like nasal congestion, facial pressure, headaches, and difficulty breathing. Acute sinusitis may last a few weeks, while chronic cases persist for months. Managing sinusitis involves staying hydrated, using steam therapy, and addressing underlying causes such as allergies or bacterial infections.
